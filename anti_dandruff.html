Anti-Dandruff Hair Treatment

Dandruff is a common condition that affects many people. It is characterized by the formation of white flakes on the scalp, which can be itchy and uncomfortable. Dandruff is not a serious medical condition, but it can be embarrassing and can affect one’s self-esteem. Effective treatments are available for dandruff at Lotus Healthcare and Aesthetics Clinic in Pune.

How to Treat Dandruff?

• Use medicated shampoos regularly as per the doctor’s advice.
• Natural home care remedies like tea tree oil Dilute tea tree oil in a carrier oil and apply it to the scalp for 30 minutes before washing it off with shampoo.

• Our specially prepared anti-dandruff hair mask and anti-dandruff hair oils are effective ways to treat dandruff.

How to Prevent Dandruff?

• Wash your hair regularly, but not too often, because over-washing can strip the scalp of its natural oils.
• Use shampoos advised by the doctor and avoid using harsh styling products that can irritate the scalp.
• Practice good hygiene, such as washing your hairbrushes and combs regularly.
• Avoid scratching the scalp, as it can worsen dandruff and lead to infections.
• Manage stress levels, as stress can contribute to dandruff.
• Follow a healthy diet and lifestyle, as poor nutrition and unhealthy habits can contribute to dandruff.
