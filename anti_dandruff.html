Dandruff is a common issue causing scalp flaking, not serious or contagious. It can be difficult to treat, but mild daily shampoo can help. It's symptoms include skin flakes on shoulders, hair, moustache, beard, scalp, and scalp itching. Symptoms worsen under stress, especially in cold, dry months.

Dandruff can be caused by an oily or dry scalp, Malassezia, hair product sensitivity, or other skin conditions like eczema and psoriasis.

Risk Elements Of Dandruff:

Dandruff is a common condition that can develop in various age groups, with males being more likely to develop it than females. Certain diseases like Parkinson's, nervous system illnesses, and HIV can increase the risk.
