BB Glow at Lotus Healthcare and Aesthetic Clinic

The BB Glow Facial Treatment is a skin-enhancing procedure which gives natural looking and radiant complexion.

This non-invasive and non-surgical procedure reduces freckles, fewer wrinkles, and brighter skin. It is available in Lotus Healthcare and Aesthetic Clinic in Pune, and is a great way to achieve a naturally glowing complexion.

A BB Glow Facial uses a 100% natural, semi-permanent foundation to remove flaws and discoloration, resulting in a light, high-quality, and lightweight complexion.

The BB Glow serum, containing niacinamide and peptides, brightens and rejuvenates the skin, providing a soft, glossy appearance and a waterproof base that won't rub off. The BB Glow Facial treatment is suitable for individuals with uneven skin tone, freckles, hyperpigmentation, acne scars, age spots, sun spots, early aging, and slack skin.

Benifits of BB Glow:

BB Glow facial treatment addresses acne marks, scars, dark spots, melasma, hyperpigmentation, dark circles, uneven skin tone, open pores, overall skin hydration, and improved skin texture and quality.

BB Glow Process:

The BB Glow facial procedure involves a deep cleanse, exfoliation, neutralization, hand-tailored booster serum, and a Bio-Silk Mask. The cleanser removes oil and dirt, the exfoliating gel gently cleans pores, and a toner restores pH. The serum is selected based on color and a hydrating mask is used.
