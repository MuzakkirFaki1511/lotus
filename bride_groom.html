Bride & Groom Treatment at Lotus Healthcare and Aesthetic Clinic

Getting married can be overwhelming with various decisions like outfits, makeup, hair, and skin. A fresh, plump, and rejuvenated skin is crucial for a unique and complimenting look.

Lotus Healthcare and Aesthetic Clinic's Bride & Groom Treatment in Pune offers individualized bridal skin care and pre-bridal packages, allowing couples to create lifelong memories in challenging situations.

Pre-bridal treatments aim to improve both inside and outside skin, preventing future deterioration. They eliminate sun damage, hyperpigmentation, and brown spots, enhance blood flow, and promote collagen formation, resulting in softer, more supple skin.

Bridal Package

Skin care starts 3-5 months before the wedding. Exfoliate your skin and use regular moisturizer. Forget the so called "bridal facials". Get smart, get scientific and visit Skiin Bliss to start with skin-peeling sessions or microdermabrasion (skin polishing) or even laser resurfacing (laser facials) for your face (depending on your budget).
They are all painless and not at all time-consuming. They improve skin color, texture and smoothen out patchy pigmentary irregularities. Smoother, even-toned skin ensures compliments and better light reflectivity of the skin ensures wonderful photographs!
