Carbon Facial at Lotus Healthcare and Aesthetic Clinic

A Carbon Facial is a skin resurfacing technique that reduces acne and improves skin texture, ideal for oily, acne-prone skin. It balances tone, minimizes sun damage, and enhances skin appearance within 30 minutes. Learn more about this minimally invasive procedure.

It is a non-invasive, instant-glow procedure that targets skin issues like hyperpigmentation, sun damage, and early aging, restoring clear, dry, and dull skin.

Carbon facial is safe for all skin types, but best for healthy or slightly damaged skin. Start with a skin rejuvenation treatment for severe damage. Repeat as needed for maintaining skin health.

Process of Carbon Facial:

A carbon facial involves coating the skin with a thin layer of carbon, then a focused light beam is used to target specific areas. This process gently removes damaged cells and absorbs thermal energy, promoting collagen production and healthy skin. This non-invasive technique is known for its noticeable results.

Carbon facial offer several benefits, including:
1. Face cleansing

3. Skin rejuvenation
4. Acne clearing 5. Skin smoothening and brightening
6. Deep skin exfoliation
7. Lifting and tightening, and reducing sagging skin.
8. The laser removes polluted carbon particles, resulting in a smoother, lighter, brighter, and more youthful complexion.
9. It also helps in acne clearing and oil removal by destroying acne-causing germs and reducing sebaceous glands' oil production capacity.
