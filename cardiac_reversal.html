Cardiac Prevention and Rehabilitation Program (CPRP)

Lotus Healthcare is a pioneer in the implementation of Personalized Cardiac Prevention and Rehabilitation Program (CPRP).
Our team of experts focuses on reducing and preventing heart health risks and revitalizing the patient's health and quality of life through Panchakarm, Ayurveda medicines, exercise and nutrition, counselling and stress management.

WHO CAN take Heart Disease Reversal/Cardiac Preventive and Rehabilitation Program (CPRP)?

1. Those who want to prevent futuristic heart disease.
2. Patients who have Coronary Heart Disease Patients who have had a heart attack.
3. Patients who have undergone angioplasty Patients who have Low Ejection Fraction (EF) in 2D Echo.
4. Patients who have undergone bypass surgery / CABG.
5. Patients with a history of cardiac arrest / heart failure.
6. Angiography patients with blockages.
7. Patients who have had a heart valve replacement.
8. Patients with stable angina pectoris.
9. Patients with pacemakers.
