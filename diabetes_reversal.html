Steps:

1. Medical Assessment And Finding A Root Cause Of Diabetes

Diabetes is a condition that can be caused by a variety of factors, including poor diet, physical inactivity, stress, and depression. The medical assessment of a Diabetic patient involves a thorough review of their medical history and family history to determine the underlying cause of their diabetes. The examination includes blood tests or other Diabetes investigations related to the affected organs. Gestational diabetes can also be diagnosed during pregnancy. Most of the time, Type 1 Diabetes is genetic in origin. Type 2 Diabetes can also be caused by poor diet and physical inactivity.

2. Integration Of Ayurveda & Allopathic Medications

The first step in a diabetes reversal program is to control & manage your sugar levels. Doctors may continue, modify or prescribe your existing allopathic medicine, Ayurveda Herbal Medicines, or in some cases both. Once the sugar level is under control, the dose of medication and insulin may get reduced and completely stopped, depending on the diabetic status of the patient. However, you should continue to measure your blood sugar regularly at home.

3. Nutrition Counselling And Personalized Diet Plan

Diet is very important for diabetics. We get energy from everyday food. However, in the case of a diabetic patient, the conversion of sugar into energy does not take place efficiently, which is why the blood sugar increases. At Lotus Holistic Healthcare, our nutritionists prepare a special and effective tailor-made diet keeping in mind the individual's dietary preferences.

4. Exercise Program, Yoga, And Meditation

Exercise is an effective way to lower blood sugar levels. During exercise, the sugar in the body is converted into energy. Our fitness experts help diabetics with an accurate exercise schedule, advising on the type and technique of exercise and the duration of exercise.
