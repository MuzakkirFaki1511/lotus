What is mesotherapy for hair?

Mesotherapy is a medically-proven treatment to address hair loss. The procedure of mesotherapy involves micro-injections by mesogun, dermapen, or insulin syringes into the scalp. This stimulates hair growth and promotes new hair production.
Mesotherapy is a minimally invasive and safe hair growth treatment. The injecting needles used are very thin to minimize the pain.

Mesotherapy hair loss treatment options are:

Microneedling Mesotherapy: This involves using a specialized mesogun device with very fine needles to deliver hair growth factors into the scalp. It stimulates blood circulation and cell metabolism.

PRP Mesotherapy: The patient’s own plasma, which is rich in growth factors, is injected into the scalp in areas with poor hair growth. PRP has regenerative properties to revive dormant follicles.

Medicinal Mesotherapy: This involves customized injections containing a cocktail of nutrients like multivitamins, amino acids, minerals, etc., tailored as per the patient’s needs. It is helpful to prevent hair fall as well as hair loss.

No needle mesotherapy: It is good for individuals who are afraid of injections or wish to take a less invasive approach to hair growth. It is painless, and it can be combined with other treatments.

How many mesotherapy sessions are needed for hair loss?

Usually, a minimum of 8 to 10 sessions are required to stimulate hair growth. We recommend starting with treatments every 2 weeks for the first 2–3 months. Then frequency gradually decreases to monthly for 2 to 3 months. Maintenan treatments are generally needed once every 2 to 3 months.
