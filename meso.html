Meso Therapy at Lotus Healthcare and Aesthetics Clinic

Meso Therapy is a non-invasive technique that uses nutrient-rich liquid to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss in alopecia. It involves injections of medications, vitamins, and plant extracts, with 6-8 sessions recommended for optimal results.

How does Meso Therapy Work?

Meso therapy is a treatment using DHT and vitamins and minerals to stimulate hair growth and stimulate new hair. It uses a special injection gun and helps stimulate blood circulation to weak hair follicles, boost metabolism, and stimulate collagen. With a success rate of 90-99%, it is a popular option for treating alopecia, baldness, and dandruff.

Hair mesotherapy is suitable for individuals experiencing severe hair loss, scalp dryness, excessive dandruff, splits, and volume loss. However, it's not suitable for those with allergies, diabetes, liver, kidney, or cardiac issues, pregnant or lactating, on anticoagulants, or have an infection.
