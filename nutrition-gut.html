Nutrition for hair:

• Vitamin B7: It is also called hair food. It is used during hair follicular growth.
• Vitamin C improves circulation on the scalp, helps iron absorption, and thus hair growth.
• Vitamin A: Controls the synthesis of retinoic acid in the hair follicle

• Vitamin E provides antioxidant protection.
• Vitamin D: It activates the hair growth genes. Its deficiency can cause diffuse hair fall, making the life span of hair follicles very short.
• Iron: Hair follicles need ferritin for growth. If iron levels are low, the ferritin from the hair follicles is taken up into serum, and severe hair fall occurs.
• Haemoglobin: If HB levels are less than 12g, then it can cause adverse effects on hair.
• Calcium: The hair follicle uses calcium to form the hair protein.
• Zinc concentrates in hair follicles and inhibits 5-alpha reductase.
• Arginine: It is a potent blood vessel dilator and can increase blood flow to the hair, thus providing oxygen and nutrients.
• Omega 3: Essential fatty acids like omega-3 fatty acids help blood circulation and hair growth. They increase nutrition absorption by the hair.
• Antioxidants: Antioxidants protect the hair from oxidative stress.
