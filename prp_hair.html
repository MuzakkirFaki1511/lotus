What Is PRP Therapy?

Platelet rich plasma is obtained when blood is centrifuged in special tube to have a high concentration of platelets. It is derived from the patient’s whole blood. This plasma is then used to treat hair loss.
It is one of the best hair loss treatment. PRP stimulate the body’s own natural healing power and increases collagen production. PRP is an effective and non-invasive treatment that helps in hair growth.
It is also called as ‘liquid gold’.

Advantages Of PRP?

PRP uses the patients ’s own blood for rejuvenation, it offers many advantages it has been used in medical treatment and cosmetic procedures.

Here are the cosmetic uses of PRP:

• Anti-ageing
• Scars
• Hair loss
• Skin rejuvenation
• Skin tightening

Are there any Side Effects Of PRP therapy?

Since PRP is a non-surgical treatment that involves patients own blood so no any significant side effects. It also minimises the risk of allergic reactions and infections.
However, you may experience the following:
Multiple pricks, so minimal discomfort and pin-pointed bleeding as it are a minimally invasive treatment. Doctor can opt for no needdle mesotherapy technique alternative to multiple pricks to reduce discomfort, pin point bleeding, and neddle fear.
Your doctor may use local anaesthesia to reduce the discomfort. You may experience soreness and redness at the site of injections.
Heaviness in the head that may subside within an hour.
However, pregnant and early lactating women should avoid PRP treatment.

When results are seen?

Generally a person can observe increase in hair strength and quality and hair growth after 4 to 6 sessions of treatment.

Is PRP Painful?

It is a minimally invasive procedure and is virtually pain-free.

What is Duration of PRP treatment?

Trichologists recommend 6-8 sessions at an interval of one month each. Each session lasts from an hour to an hour and a half.
