PRP treatment with Lotus Healthcare and Aesthetics

PRP treatment involves drawing, processing, and injecting blood into the scalp to treat hair loss. Platelets, a first responder in the body, are extracted and injected into damaged areas to speed up healing. PRP has gained popularity for restoring hair growth.

How does PRP Treatment Work?

PRP treatment involves drawing blood from a patient's arm vein, spinning it in a centrifuge, processing it, extracting platelets, and injecting them into specific scalp areas. The procedure can take up to an hour and may require multiple sessions.

After a hair treatment, use pH-balanced shampoo for three days, wait 6 hours before using hair products, avoid saunas, steam rooms, swimming, vigorous exercise, sun, and heat exposure for two days.
