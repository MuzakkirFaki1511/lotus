The Panchakarma therapy at Lotus Healthcare & Aesthetics includes:

1. Vamana

Vamana, one of the five treatments in Panchakarma, focuses on expelling excessive Kapha Dosha from the body. This therapy induces therapeutic vomiting, eliminating excess mucus through the gastrointestinal and respiratory routes. Kapha is primarily located in the chest region, and its accumulation can lead to ailments such as coughs, colds, and respiratory issues.

Benefits of Vamana: Vamana therapy can improve voice quality, enhance appetite, and is particularly beneficial for conditions like asthma, diabetes, and certain skin problems such as psoriasis. It effectively clears sinuses, relieves congestion, eases wheezing, and alleviates breathlessness.

2. Virechana, Vasti, Nasya, and Raktamokshana

In addition to Vamana, Panchakarma includes other treatments such as Virechana (purging), Vasti (enema), Nasya (nasal therapy), and Raktamokshana (bloodletting). Each of these treatments serves a unique purpose, addressing various imbalances in the body and promoting overall wellness.
