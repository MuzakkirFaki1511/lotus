The Executive and IT Professional Rejuvenation Package

Our Executive and IT Professional Rejuvenation Package is a comprehensive offering that addresses the specific health concerns of those in high-demand professions. The Package Includes:

1. Abhyangam (30 minutes)

Abhyangam is a traditional Ayurvedic massage that uses herbal oils. It's a therapeutic massage designed to improve circulation, relieve muscle tension, and promote overall relaxation. The 30-minute session is a perfect break from the hustle and bustle of your daily work life.

2. Herbal Steam Bath (15 minutes)

A herbal steam bath is an excellent way to detoxify your body and cleanse your skin. The steam opens up your pores, allowing the herbs to penetrate deeply. This short but effective treatment is ideal for executives and IT professionals who need a quick yet rejuvenating experience.

3. Netra Trapanam (20 minutes)

Netra Trapanam is an eye rejuvenation therapy that involves placing a pool of medicated oil around the eyes. It helps alleviate eye strain, irritation, and dryness often experienced by those who spend long hours in front of screens. The 20-minute session brings relief to tired eyes.
