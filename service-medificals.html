Unveil Your Radiance: The Power of Medifacials at Lotus Healthcare & Aesthetics

Introduction

In a world where maintaining vibrant and healthy skin is a timeless aspiration, Medifacials emerge as a game-changer.

While traditional facials promise temporary radiance, Medifacials offer a lasting, natural rejuvenation that redefines skincare.

At Lotus Healthcare & Aesthetics, we take pride in introducing you to the world of Medifacials, designed to cater to your unique skin type and needs.

The Medifacial Advantage

Medifacials are more than just a cosmetic procedure; they are a gateway to long-lasting beauty and skin health. Unlike regular facials, which deliver fleeting results, Medifacials offer a profound transformation. Our expert team at Lotus Healthcare & Aesthetics brings you a range of Medifacials tailored to address specific concerns, each harnessing the power of Ayurveda and modern wellness science.

1. Radiance Facial

Designed to preserve natural beauty and skin health, the Radiance Facial is particularly effective for dry, dehydrated, sensitive, and prematurely aging skin. It also helps combat the effects of sun damage. This Medifacial can be applied to the face, neck, back, and hands, and is recommended for monthly sessions lasting between 30 minutes to 1 hour.

2. Acne Facial

Perfect for those dealing with mild acne, acne-prone skin, and oily complexions. The Acne Facial focuses on cleansing and rebalancing the skin, leaving it clear and refreshed. Recommended every 15-30 days for a duration of 45 minutes.

3. Rehydra Facial

Dehydrated skin can now find solace in the Rehydra Facial. This Medifacial is specifically designed to hydrate dry and dehydrated skin, making it an excellent choice for individuals exposed to constant humidity or suffering from shaving rash. Suitable for the face, it's advised for monthly sessions lasting between 45 minutes to 1 hour.

4. Detox Facial

Revitalize your skin's natural health with the Detox Facial. This treatment effectively combats the effects of smoking, stress, constant traveling, and weight loss on the skin. It's a one-hour session recommended on a monthly basis.

5. De-tan Facial

For those yearning to restore tanned skin, erase dark circles, and eliminate superficial pigment spots, the De-tan Facial is the solution. This comprehensive treatment extends to the face, back, neck, and hands. Tan removal may require three sessions, with a post-Chemical Peel recommended once every two months. Each session is approximately one hour.

6. Anti-Ageing Facial

Designed to counteract the signs of aging, preserve skin's natural health, and revive dull and rough skin. This one-hour session is recommended once a month for lasting benefits.
