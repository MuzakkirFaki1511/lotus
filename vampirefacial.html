Vampire Facial at Lotus Healthcare and Aesthetic Clinic

Vampire facial uses microneedling and platelet-rich plasma therapy to stimulate cell turnover, increase collagen and elastin production, and stimulate cell turnover, resulting in firmer, younger-looking skin.

Vampire facial treatment is suitable for individuals with premature wrinkles, sun damage, even-toned complexion, acne scars, and dull skin, but not for those with bleeding disorders or blood diseases.

A vampire facial is a lunch time procedure that involves cleansing the skin, applying numbing cream, drawing blood, extracting plasma-rich platelets, applying them to the skin through microneedling. This procedure requires minimal downtime and can be resumed the next day.

Key Benefits:

A vampire facial offers non-invasive benefits such as improved wrinkle appearance, enhanced skin texture, plumper complexion, rejuvenated skin, restored youthful glow, reduced pores size, and increased skincare product effectiveness.
